Silver tier users of JuanCash are eligible to apply for JuanCash Card.

Apply JuanCash Card through JuanCash app. You will get the card for only Php 265, ensure you have sufficient balance in your JuanCash wallet upon card application.

Step 1. Go to 'Profile' then click JuanCash Card
Step 2. Click 'Apply for JuanCash Card'
Step 3. Fill out required user information and upload valid ID
Step 4. Click activate verification to get SMS verification code
Step 5. Click 'I have read and agree Terms of Service'
Step 6. Fill out your residential address and billing address
Step 7. Your JuanCash App have atleast 265Php for JuanCash Card fee inclusive of shipping fee in order to proceed.
Step 8. Click 'PREPAID CARD MEMBERSHIP P265.00' to submit
Step 9. then your application will be sent for approval
Step 10. Your card will be deliver to you after you have been approved.

Your JuanCash Card can be used anywhere MasterCard and Bancnet is accepted. You can use your card to pay for purchases made at a store and online. You can also use your card for ATM transactions through AUB ATMs or any Bancnet ATMs or MasterCard ATMs if you are outside the country

Whatever balance you have in the wallet is the same balance you can use for your card transaction. That means, you load your JuanCash wallet, not the Card. You can load your JuanCash wallet via Cash-in methods such as Online banking, over the counter, Business center, and other stores like 7-11 and remittance centers
