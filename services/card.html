Introducing more ways to use your JuanCash stored money value worldwide!

JuanCash Card is seamlessly integrated with AUB Mastercard. Simply tap and pay wherever Mastercard is accepted globally.

Steps on how to apply JuanCash Card on your mobile app

1. Go to Profile then click JuanCash Card

2. Click 'Apply for JuanCash Card'

Note: You have to be in Silver Tier in order to apply.

3. Fill out required user information and upload valid ID

4. click activate verification to get SMS verification code

5. click 'I have read and agree Terms of Service'

6. Fill out your residential address and billing address

7. your JuanCash App have atleast 265Php for JuanCash Card fee (inclusive of shipping fee) in order to proceed.

8. click 'PREPAID CARD MEMBERSHIP P265.00' to submit

9. then your application will be sent for approval

10. Your card will be deliver after you have been approved.
