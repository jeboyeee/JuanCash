Risks

The following sections describe risks associated with (but not unique to) virtual currency use and trading. Zybi Tech bears no responsibility for protecting the customer against these risks.

Price volatility

The Customer must be aware that the prices of virtual currencies may surge or drop precipitously at any time. This may be affected by factors such as (but not limited to):

  • Changing supply and demand
  • Political events
  • Economic events
  • Wars
  • Disasters
  • Changes to regulations governing the virtual currency market

Compromised accounts, passwords, or keys

Virtual currency wallets have public and private keys, both consisting of symbols and characters. The public key can be shared with other users, it allows the user to receive cryptocurrencies into his or her digital wallet. Private keys, on the other hand, must always be safeguarded. If a private key is lost, the user will not be able to withdraw from his own account. If a private key is compromised, unauthorized actors can gain control or steal the contents of the virtual currency wallet. While Zybi Tech has support processes that can assist a Customer in gaining back control of compromised assets, Zybi Tech is not liable for any loses caused by compromised accounts, passwords, or keys.
