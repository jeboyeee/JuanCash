1. Customer - Refers to JuanCash and JuanExchange users whose personal, sensitive personal, or privileged information is processed. The term "You" and "Your" shall refer to Customer.
2. Personal Data - refers to all type of personal information and sensitive personal information.
3. 5. Processing - Refers to any operation or any set of operations performed upon personal data including, but not limited to, the collection, recording, organization, storage, updating, or modification, retrieval, consultation, use, consolidation, blocking, erasure or distraction of data.

Who we are?

Established on July 23, 2018, Zybi Tech Inc. Doing business under the name and style of Juancash, is a forward-looking Financial Technology ("FinTech") company that aims to provide financial services to the unbanked and underbanked; and to bridge the gap between virtual currency and the Filipinos by providing easy-to-use and costâ€”effective products and services.

On September 06, 2018, the Company initially secured its Provisional Certificate of Registration ("PCOR") from the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas ("BSP") to operate as a Remittance and Transfer Company ("RTC") with Virtual Currency Exchange ("VCE"). Then, on May 21, 2019, the Company secured another PCOR from BSP to operate as RTC with an Electronic Money Issuer ("EMI") with VCE.

On May 29, 2019, the Company unveiled JuanCash and JuanExchange in a soft launch in Makati City, Philippines. JuanCash functions the EMI; it is a mobile e-wallet application where the customers can transfer funds, send/receive remittance, perform cashless withdrawal, buy airtime load, and pay utility bills; while JuanExchange functions the VCE; it is a web-based platform that provides facility for, and engages in activities relating to, conversion or exchange of fiat currency to virtual currency or vice versa. The choice of the term "Juan" in the platforms, JuanCash and JuanExchange, is the personification of the Filipinos as the Company was founded by five (5) patriotic and nationalistic Filipino individuals.

On July 22, 2019, the Company formally introduced JuanCash and JuanExchange to the market in a grand launch at Century Park Hotel in Manila City, Philippines. On September 09, 2019, the BSP formally issued a Certificate of Registration ("COR) in favor of the Company.
