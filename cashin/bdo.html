How to Cash-in using BDO Mobile App

From BDO to JuanCash

1. Log in to your BDO Personal Online Banking or Mobile Banking Account

2. Choose Send Money >> To Another Local Bank

3. Select from saved request or proceed without a template

4. Complete the following details:
- Transaction Type
- Enter Amount
- Choose from Destination Bank
- Enter Destination Account Number, Last Name, First Name, Middle Initial, Address 1 & Address 2
- Registered Number in your JuanCash App
*Add "63" or the country code before the Registered Number
(i.e., 639-XXXX-XXXXX | 861-XXXX-XXXX-XX)
- Registered Name in your JuanCash App

5. Choose JuanCash International 12 Digits via InstaPay[ZTI12]
(for Philippines only otherwise choose accordingly)

6. Enter the One-Time-Pin sent to your phone or generated by your BDO Mobile App's OTP Generator

7. Confirm details

8. You will be notified via SMS that transaction is successful

*BDO transaction fees may apply
*Minimum add funds amount is Php100
*Maximum add funds amount is Php10,000 per transaction
