How to Cash-in using UnionBank

From Unionbank to JuanCash

1. Log in to your UnionBank

2. Select Send Money

3. Choose Other Banks or E-Wallets

4. Choose between the following Fund Transfer Channels:

- InstaPay: Real-time Fund Transfer up to P50,000
- PESONet: Same day Fund Transfer before 3:00 PM. Transactions after 3:00PM will be processed next banking day

5. Complete the following details:

- bank or an e-wallet: JuanCash
- Registered Number in your JuanCash App*
- Enter Account Name*
- Enter Email Address

6. Enter Amount to Send

7. Confirm Details and tap Next

*UnionBank transaction fees may apply
