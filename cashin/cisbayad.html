How to Cash-in using CIS Bayad App.

From CIS Bayad App to JuanCash

1. Log in to your CIS Bayad App.

2. Tap Send Money.

3. Tap To Banks and Wallets.

4. Enter your Account details:

- Account Holder Name: Your JuanCash registered name.
- Select Bank: JuanCash.
- Account No.: Enter your JuanCash registered mobile number and follow this format:
*Add "63" or the country code under Destination Account Number
(i.e., 639-XXXX-XXXXX | 861-XXXX-XXXX-XX)

5. Go to the Fund Transfer and fill in the details:

- Select Source Account
- Target Account
- Amount
- Remarks

6. Confirm your information then tap Confirm.

7. Enter the One Time Pin (OTP) that will be sent to your registered mobile number.

8. SMS will be sent to the receiver.

*CIS Bayad Banking transaction fees may apply
