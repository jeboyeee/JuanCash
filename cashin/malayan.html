How to Cash-in using Malayan Savings Bank app

From Malayan Savings Bank app to JuanCash

1. Log in to your Malayan Savings Bank account.

2. Tap Transfer.

3. Enter your account details:

- Source: Source Account
- Amount: Enter desired amount
- Bank: JuanCash
- Account Name: Your JuanCash registered name
- Account No.: Enter your JuanCash registered mobile number and follow this format:
*Add "63" or the country code under Destination Account Number
(i.e., 639-XXXX-XXXXX | 861-XXXX-XXXX-XX)

4. Confirm the transfer details.

*Malayan Savings Bank transaction fees may apply
