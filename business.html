Individual Reseller

JuanCash Individual Reseller can earn extra profit as compared to regular users, like selling e-load, bills payment, and remittances. Individual Reseller will also earn Php500 commissions for every successful user that succesfully joined the individual reseller program simply by referring users using reseller invitation code.

Paano mag-register?

1. I-tap ang "Profile".

2. I-tap ang "Reseller Program" icon

3. Ilagay ang "invitation code".

4. I-tap ang SIGN THE AGREEMENT ONLINE

5. I-enter ang iyong 6-digit Payment Password. (kung wala kang code gamitin ang code na ito "p3y9991")

NOTE: Siguraduhing may laman ang iyong JuanCash wallet na halagang PHP1,000

How to claim your PHP1,000 cashback registration fee?

1. This program only applies to those successful JuanCash Reseller who used the Zybi Tech Inc's invitation code of "p3y9991". Referral code used other than the Zybi Tech Inc invitation shall not be entitled for cashback registration fee.

2. Take a screenshot of your JuanCash Reseller landing page screen; and

3. Send your full name, mobile number and screenshot to juancash_support@zybitech.com using the subject "JuanCash Reseller Program".

*your registration fee will be refunded within 3 business working days.

Requirements

1.   At least 18 years old to be a JuanCash Individual Reseller

2.   Andriod or IOS smartphone.

3.   Download the JuanCash App.

4.   Must be a "Silver Tier" user.

Merchant

Become our merchant and Boost your business by diversifying your payment options via our latest QR technology.

Steps on how to apply as a Merchant

1. Download JuanCash App
(For new merchants, they will no longer go through Bronze and Silver application just Direct to Register corporate account.)

2. Tap "Register corporate account"

3. Enter Mobile Number and tap "Next"

4. Enter verification code and tap "Next"

5. check "I agree to the Terms of services" and tap "Next"

6. Fill up you "Merchant Application"

  • Create your store
  • Choose your business type
    • Sole proprietors (small and micro ENT)
    • Sole proprietors (SME)
    • Partnership
    • Company
  • Input promoter/agent ID (not required)
  • Input your mobile phone number
  • Input your email
  • Input your address
  • Upload Store Logo
  • Upload Store front photos
  • Upload In-store photos

7. Merchant Application - Upload nessary Certification documents

8. Tap "Apply for download Customer Information Sheet, Signed Terms and Conditions"

9. Check Document Application

  • Customer Data Sheet
  • Signed terms and conditions
  • Enter Email

10. Tap "Application"

11. Then tap "Finish"
