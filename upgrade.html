Upgrade account into Silver Tier

How to upgrade my account into Silver Tier?

Step 1. Go to JuanCash App Profile and choose 'Upgrade Account'. Click the 'Upgrade to Silver' and complete the requirements.

Step 2. Fill in your personal information. Upload your ID photo with signature and take a personal photo.

Step 3. Record yourself and follow the instructions. Review the information before submitting your application.

Step 4. Wait for the confirmation of your upgrade. Once approved, you may now use JuanCash services such as Bills Payment and many more.
